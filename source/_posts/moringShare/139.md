**It was an accident**

- What's that for? 
- Sorry, it was an accident.
- What did you do that for? It really hurts.
- Like I say, it was an accident.
- 为什么打我？
- 抱歉，我不是故意的。
- 你打我干什么？很痛。
- 我说了啊，不是故意的。

**I didn't mean to do that**
- 在口语中也可以省略为I didn't mean to

- Mom! Dad! Luke hit me.
- I didn't mean to.
- 妈妈，爸爸，卢克打到了我。
- 我不是故意的。

**I didn't do it on purpose**

- What you have to remember is that he's not doing it on purpose. It's just how he is.
- 你要知道，他不是故意的。只是他这人天性如此。 -->
